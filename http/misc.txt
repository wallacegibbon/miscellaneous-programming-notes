## vim: ft=conf :

## a basic form looks like:
<form action="form_action.asp" method="get">
<input type="submit" value="Submit" />
</form>

## besides "action" and "method", form have a interesting parameter: enctype

## enctype can be one of them: (default `application/x-www-form-urlencoded`)
application/x-www-form-urlencoded
multipart/form-data
text/plain
